5. Data Storage & Security

Where Is Your Data Stored?

  • Database: MongoDB Atlas cloud servers (hosted in Asia Pacific region)
  • Images: Cloudinary servers (global CDN)
  • On your device: Your login tokens are stored locally in the app's secure storage (AsyncStorage)

How We Protect Your Data

  • Passwords: Bcrypt hashing with salt â€” we never store plain-text passwords
  • Login sessions: JWT tokens with 15-minute expiry + 7-day refresh tokens
  • Two-factor authentication: OTP required on every login
  • HTTPS: All data transmission encrypted in production
  • Role-based access: Each user can only access data appropriate to their role
  • College isolation: Admins can only access their own college's data
  • Rate limiting: Login attempts limited to 10 per 15 minutes
  • Security headers: Helmet.js protects against common web attacks

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You can view all your personal data within the app (Profile, Attendance, Results, etc.).

Right to Correct

You can update your name, phone number, and profile photo directly in the app. Contact your college admin to update department, semester, or enrollment details.

Right to Delete

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact your college administrator or email us at ayusht1i2w3@gmail.com. We will process deletion within 30 days.

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can log out of the app at any time, which clears your local session data. To stop using the service, contact your admin to deactivate your account.

Right to Data Portability

You can request a copy of your attendance records and academic data by contacting your college administrator.

To exercise any of these rights: Contact your college administrator directly, or email us at ayusht1i2w3@gmail.com. We respond within 7 business days.

7. Children's Privacy

COLLAHUB is designed for use by college students who are typically 17 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at ayusht1i2w3@gmail.com. We will delete such information within 30 days of being notified.

Student accounts are created by college administrators, not self-registered by minors. This provides an additional layer of oversight.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Mobile App

The COLLAHUB mobile app does not use browser cookies. We use AsyncStorage (device-local secure storage) to store your login session tokens. This data stays on your device and is cleared when you log out.

Web Version

If you use COLLAHUB via web browser, we may use session storage to maintain your login. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

What We Do NOT Use

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking
  • Facebook Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Advertising networks
  • Cross-site tracking
