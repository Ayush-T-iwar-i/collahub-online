§ Features

Everything in one place.

No more paper registers, scattered WhatsApp groups, or 4 different Excel sheets. Run your entire college from a single, well-mannered app.

✅
Smart Attendance
Track Theory and Lab attendance separately. A color-coded date navigator shows marked and unmarked days at a glance.
Learn more ↗
📅
Timetable Management
4-layer conflict detection prevents teacher, room, batch, and subject clashes automatically.
Learn more ↗
🔄
Class Sharing
Substitute teacher feature lets faculty delegate classes. Access expires when the period ends.
Learn more ↗
🪪
Digital ID Cards
QR enabled identity cards for students and teachers. Download as PNG straight from the app.

OTP Authentication
