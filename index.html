Features

Everything in one place

No more paper registers or Excel sheets. Manage your entire college from a single app.

✅
Smart Attendance
Track Theory and Lab attendance separately. Color-coded date navigator shows marked and unmarked days at a glance.
📅
Timetable Management
4-layer conflict detection prevents teacher, room, batch, and subject clashes automatically.
🔄
Class Sharing
Substitute teacher feature lets faculty delegate classes. Access expires automatically when the class period ends.
🪪
Digital ID Cards
QR code enabled identity cards for students and teachers. Download as PNG directly from the app.
🔒
OTP Authentication
Secure email-verified login with JWT access tokens and automatic refresh token rotation.
🏫
Multi-College Support
One platform for multiple institutions. Super Admin manages all colleges from a single dashboard.
